Frequently asked questions

If you can't find what you're looking for, contact us: hello@kleedesktop.com

What is Klee?
Klee is a note and knowledge base app with client-side AI models.
Is Klee free?
Yes, Klee is totally free if you are in private mode and run it on your local machine.
How does Klee ensure data security?
In private mode, Klee runs entirely on your local machine, meaning your data never leaves your device, thus eliminating the risk of data breaches or leaks.
Can Klee handle team collaboration?
To be released, Klee supports team-shared knowledge bases and offers admin and user roles to facilitate collaboration.
What features does Klee offer for large enterprises?
To be released, custom AI model training and deployment, team-shared knowledge bases and admin/user roles.
How does Klee assist with file and knowledge base management?
Klee can scan and summarize individual files and entire knowledge bases, providing comprehensive insights through conversational interactions.
Can Klee work on top of Ollama?
Yes, Klee can work on top of Ollama, providing a seamless experience for users who prefer to use Ollama as their LLM provider.
Can Klee work with OpenAI and Anthropic?
Yes, Klee offers cloud mode with OpenAI and Anthropic services.
How can I get support for Klee?
If you need assistance, you can email our support team, and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.
